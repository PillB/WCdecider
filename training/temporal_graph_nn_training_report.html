Training, Testing & Finetuning ProcessProceso de Entrenamiento, Prueba y Ajuste Fino

Data & Splits
  • • Dataset A: Historical backtest (WC 2018/2022 + 2023-26 friendlies/WCQ, N≈207)
  • • Dataset B: Elapsed WC2026 matches + June 17-21 slate
  • • Temporal CV: Strict chronological walk-forward (pre-2024 → early 2026 → mid-2026)
  • • Train/Val/Test: 70/15/15 chrono split inside TGNN head training
Architectures Tested
  • • v4.1_ensemble (baseline, production)
  • • TGNN variants: emb_dim = [4,8,16], temporal_only vs full_graph (message passing + GRU-style memory)
  • • Ensemble blends: 75/25, 50/50, SPEC-heavy
  • • TGN-inspired: memory module + time-decayed opponent messages + graph aggregation (see wc_temporal_graph_nn.py)
Key Finetunes & Decisions
  • • Champion = 75% v4.1 + 25% TGNN_d8_fullgraph (best Brier + adaptation + 0 traps)
  • • Rule 27 compliance: trap_count=0 on MOD favorites for all candidates
  • • Research references: Rossi et al. TGN (2020), TGB benchmark, PyG-Temporal, soccer pass TGN papers
  • • Output: Updated wc_june17_21_predictions.json + visualizations in this report

Interpretations (3 levels)Interpretaciones (3 niveles)

Junior: The blend of old reliable v4.1 with new graph models (TGNN + simple GraphMixer) gives better or equal accuracy on real games without taking crazy risks (no 'trap' bets on heavy favorites). Good for conservative decisions.El blend del confiable v4.1 con nuevos modelos de grafo da mejor o igual precisión en partidos reales sin riesgos locos (sin apuestas trampa en favoritos pesados). Bueno para decisiones conservadoras.
Mid: Temporal CV curves show TGNN/GraphMixer adapt as more history arrives (Brier drops in later folds thanks to memory + neighbor messages). Stacking adds diversity; simple GraphMixer (no complex attention) matches complex models per 2023 ICLR paper - less overfitting risk. Champion 70% v4 keeps discipline (Rule 27) while 30% new adds edge on temporal signals.Curvas CV temporal muestran que TGNN/GraphMixer se adaptan con más historia (Brier baja en pliegues tardíos por memoria + mensajes de vecinos). Stacking añade diversidad; GraphMixer simple iguala modelos complejos (paper ICLR23) - menos riesgo de sobreajuste. Campeón 70% v4 mantiene disciplina mientras 30% nuevo añade ventaja en señales temporales.
Expert: Per TGN survey (Zheng 2025) + GraphMixer ablation (Cong): memory + mean-pool sufficient for many dynamic link/outcome tasks; RNN/attn not always needed (GraphMixer beats TGN on some TGB). Ensemble theory (stacking papers): meta on heterogeneous bases (graph vs tabular vs Elo) reduces variance. Caveats: temporal non-stat (form shifts - use recent weighting); cold teams (mean pool helps); leakage avoided by chrono. Preponderance: hybrid graph+traditional + small dynamic MoE is robust yet updatable. Full metrics + preds in training/*.json. Preponderancia evidencia: híbrido grafo+tradicional + MoE dinámico pequeño es robusto y actualizable. Métricas completas en training/*.json.
