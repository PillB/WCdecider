ELI5 — How to place these bets in Betsson / Betano (for complete beginners)
What is “1X2”? 1 = home/team A wins, X = draw, 2 = away/team B wins. Decimal odds 1.87 means if you bet S/100 and win you get back S/187 total (100 stake + 87 profit).
Step-by-step for Canada −1 @1.87 (Betsson example):
1. Open Betsson app (orange “b” logo bottom tab). Home tab is the house icon.
2. Tap “World Cup” or “Fútbol” / “Partidos” section (top or sports menu).
3. Find “Canada vs Qatar” (18 junio or date). Tap the match card.
4. Scroll or tap “Handicap” or “Asian” markets. Look for the tile that says “Canada −1” or “Canadá −1” with price 1.87.
5. Tap the 1.87 tile. It adds to your boletoBet slip / shopping cart for bets. Shows your selections + potential payout..
6. In the boleto at bottom, enter stake e.g. “25” (use your bankroll rules, never more than 1–1.5%). “Ganancia potencial” = total money you receive if it wins (stake + profit).
7. Review: correct match, correct selection “Canada −1”, correct odds 1.87, stake 25. Tap “Realizar apuesta” or “Confirmar”.
8. If odds move >5% before you confirm → STOP, do not chase. Screenshot the slip + balance before/after.
Same flow on Betano (red “B”, “BB BOOST”, bottom tabs Inicio / Deportes).
Never bet more than you can afford to lose. This is education only.
