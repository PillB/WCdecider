- Model selection and calibration must use chronological folds with no full-sample selection leakage; missing required weights are fatal.

- Missing odds never become estimated prices: fixtures without a real supported market price receive probability/fair-price analysis only.
- Combo probabilities must come from a joint score distribution and exact conjunction settlement, never naive multiplication of marginal probabilities.
- Betsson handicap-plus-total headers apply the displayed signed handicap to selection 1/home and the opposite handicap to selection 2/away; this rule is supported by visually checked negative and positive examples and must remain source-documented.
