COLUMN DEFINITIONS AND PROVENANCE
---------------------------------
match_id       — Unique key: WC_2018_GROUP_NNN | WC_2022_GROUP_NNN | FD_YYYYMMDD_NNNN | WC26_NNN
date           — Kickoff DD/MM/YYYY. Source: football-data Date field or FIFA schedule
competition    — WC_2018_GROUP | WC_2022_GROUP | WC_2026_GROUP | FRIENDLY | WC_QUALIFIER
comp_weight    — Rule 26 weight: WC=1.0, WCQ=0.85, FRIENDLY=0.60. Source: AGENT.md §14
team_a         — FIFA 3-letter code. Source: TEAM_NAME_MAP in loader (football-data names)
team_b         — FIFA 3-letter code. Same as team_a
team_a_name    — Display name from football-data Home or embedded WC tuple
team_b_name    — Display name from football-data Away or embedded WC tuple
elo_a_pre      — Pre-match Elo for team A BEFORE this fixture
  - WC 2018/2022: eloratings.net tournament snapshot (static per match in loader)
  - football-data rows: walk-forward K=20 from ELO_SNAPSHOT init (no look-ahead)
  - WC 2026: eloratings.net 2026-06-15 snapshot (wc_model_v3.ELO)
  - Provenance column: source_elo
elo_b_pre      — Pre-match Elo for team B. Same rules as elo_a_pre
outcome        — A (team_a win) | D (draw) | B (team_b win). Derived from final score
score          — Final score "H-A" format. Source: HG-AG or embedded WC results
total_goals    — Integer sum of goals. Derived from score
o_win_a        — Decimal odds team A win. See source_odds column
o_draw         — Decimal odds draw
o_win_b        — Decimal odds team B win
ha             — Home advantage Elo overlay (0.0 for neutral internationals in this dataset)
mu             — Expected total goals prior for Poisson leg (default 2.25). Source: v4 calibration
finetune       — Rule finetune string (e.g. "Rule 21 opener/minnow/rotation" for ESP-CPV)
source_result  — Row-level citation for match result/score
source_odds    — Row-level citation for closing odds
source_elo     — Row-level citation for Elo method (snapshot vs walk_forward_k20)

PROCESSING STEPS (in order)
---------------------------
Step 1: Fetch football-data.co.uk CSV via urllib (live URL above)
Step 2: Map team names → FIFA codes via TEAM_NAME_MAP
Step 3: Filter rows with valid scores and odds > 1.0
Step 4: Classify competition (FRIENDLY vs WC_QUALIFIER heuristic by month)
Step 5: Sort chronologically; apply walk-forward Elo (K=20, draw=0.5) — predictions use PRE-match Elo only
Step 6: Append embedded WC 2018/2022 tuples with tournament Elo snapshots
Step 7: Append WC 2026 MD1-3 from AGENT backtest with screenshot odds
Step 8: Deduplicate by (date, team_a, team_b)
Step 9: Write CSV

WHY walk-forward Elo for friendlies?
  - Prevents look-ahead bias: post-match rating updates cannot contaminate pre-match predictions
  - Required by Rule 25 expanded backtest mandate (AGENT.md)
